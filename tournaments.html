Tournament Philosophy

Our competitive pathway

PYO tournaments focus on confidence, communication, resilience, leadership, growth, and competitive excellence. Every student competes at their own level and advances when ready.

Beginner Level

Local & Introductory

Local invitationals and introductory showcases. Perfect for first-time competitors building confidence and experience.

Intermediate Level

Regional Competitions

Regional competitions and structured debates. Students face stronger competition and develop competitive strategies.

Advanced Level

National Championships

National qualifiers and championship tournaments including ASDA Nationals, NSDA, and USA Forensics Olympiad.

Events Explained

Competition events we compete in

Original Oratory

A prepared speech on a topic of the student's choice. Tests research, writing, and delivery skills.

Impromptu

Speak on a randomly assigned topic with minimal preparation. Tests quick thinking and adaptability.

Debate

Structured argumentation on a resolution. Students argue both sides, developing logic and persuasion.

SPAR

Spontaneous Argumentation — fast-paced debate with a partner on random topics. A crowd favorite.

Declamation

Deliver a famous historical speech with your own interpretation, expression, and stage presence.

Interpretation

Bring a piece of literature to life through expressive, dramatic oral reading and performance.
